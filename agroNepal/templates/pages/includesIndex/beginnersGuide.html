Vegitable Garderning for Beginners

1 Select The Right Soil

We need to select the right soil for the particular plant to ensure healthy growth and nutrient supply.

2 Select Quality Seeds

Use certified, disease-free, and high-yielding seeds suitable for your climate and region.

3 Manage Water

Provide consistent watering. Avoid overwatering or underwatering to prevent plant stress.

4 Use Organic Fertilization

Feed your plants with compost or organic fertilizers to promote sustainable, chemical-free gardening.

5 Harvest at Right Stage

Harvest vegetables at their peak maturity to enjoy the best taste and nutritional value.
